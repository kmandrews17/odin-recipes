Smoked Pulled Pork

Ingredients

  • 7-9 Pound Boston butt
  • Kosher Salt
  • Meat Church - Honey Pork Seasoning

Directions

  1. Trim Fat cap of pork butt, leaving about a quarter inch of fat
  2. Liberally season with Kosher salt, ensuring to cover all sides
  3. Heavily season with Honey Pork seasoning
  4. Put in Refridgerator uncovered overnight to let seasoning soak into the meat
  5. Take out of fridge and let it come up to room temperature and preheat smoker to 250 F
  6. Smoke Fat Cap up for 10-12 hours or until probe tender around 203 F.
